Playlist Expand/Collapse Behavior

When viewing playlists from a channel, how should the "dropdown" work?

A

Inline Expand

Clicking a playlist expands it in-place to show its videos underneath, indented. You can select the whole playlist (checkbox on the playlist row) or expand and cherry-pick individual videos. Like a file tree.

B

Navigate Into Playlist

Clicking a playlist pushes a new screen showing its videos (like the video selection screen). A "Back" button returns to the playlist list. Selecting the whole playlist = selecting it from the list without clicking in.
